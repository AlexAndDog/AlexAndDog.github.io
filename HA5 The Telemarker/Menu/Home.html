About BC

British Colombia

Known for the mountains towering high all over the province.

The Skiing,

Known for having some worlds best skiing and snowboarding British Colombia peaks on most skiers bucket list. Hosting resorts like Whistler, Revelstoke and Fernie, BC isn't for the faint-hearted.

The climbing,

The epicentre of outdoor rock climbing, British Columbia is renowned for its legendary crags like Squamish and the hidden gems in towns like Penticton. The granite cliffs of Squamish are a magnet for climbers worldwide, but it's certainly not the only destination. Explore beyond the beaten path, and you'll discover a world of climbing adventures in the rugged landscapes of BC, from the coastal ranges to the Kootenays.

The Riding,

Home of Mountain Biking, BC is home to the infamous North Shore, as well as the other small towns like Kamloops. However, the place where all mountain bikers far and wide go is Whistler, but it's definitely not the only place to go so where else?
